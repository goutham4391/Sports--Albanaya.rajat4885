Holly McLean Wins National Amateur Championship

Student-athlete Holly McLean is the 2022 Champion Golfer after winning the Cayman Islands National Amateur Championship.


Holly played great golf in very challenging winds shooting +3 in the first round which put her in the lead going into the final round. Holly shot a marvelous 2-under-par in the final round to secure the win.


Congratulations Holly on this achievement that was made possible by your hard work on and off the course.
